I am a PhD scholar at Kohat University of Science and Technology, with a research background spanning digital marketing, consumer behaviour, entrepreneurship, and organisational psychology. My published work appears in peer-reviewed journals across management and social sciences.

Beyond academia, I am an experienced academic writer and business plan developer. I help students, researchers, and entrepreneurs transform complex ideas into polished, publication-ready documents — whether that means a journal article, a thesis chapter, or a full investor-ready business plan.
